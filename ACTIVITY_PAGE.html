Win the ball, score a goal

In matches there are all sorts of situations that face a player - 1v1,
2v2, 3v3 and so on. The activity is how to make the attacking players to take advantage of these situations to win the ball and fire a shot at goal.

Five minute warm up

Get to the opposite group - go when your number is called.
This is a great warm-up to get the players ready for passing and receiving in a match.
MORE





Transition success

In the professional game there are around 200 turnovers of the ball in every match,
so players need to practice how to deal with them.
Reacting to turnover balls is vital at youth level because often teams are switched off to the positives and negatives.
MORE





Four square - press the pack

This is a great session for getting players to pass with accuracy and speed
when under pressure which means on match days they are able to keep hold of the ball when pressed by the opposition.
